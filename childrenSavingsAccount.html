Child Savings Account (CSA)
Savings & Investments > Children Saving Account

Enables parents/guardians to plan and save for their children’s future from ages 0-18 years on the attainment of medium and long-term goals.

Features

  • Interest payable on your monthly deposit. Base rate plus 1.5% per annum payable quarterly
  • Withdrawal is not expected more than once a quarter otherwise the 2.5% interest is forfeited
  • Account automatically converts to mortgage plan savings account at age 18
  • Minimum opening balance of N5,000
  • Minimum balance in account is N5,000
  • Minimum monthly deposit of N10,000
